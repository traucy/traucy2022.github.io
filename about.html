Hi~! I’m Tracy, a product designer moving people and pixels at Daybreak.

My work is driven by research-informed decisions and experimentation. I value understanding people and businesses in order to create an ecosystem of holistic interventions. How people are moved by a product, how pixels are layed out, and the details that form each experience are what matters most to me.

I graduated with a B.A degree at SFU’s School of Interactive Arts and Technology in Vancouver, Canada. My design practices include UI/visual design, interaction design, strategic thinking, prototyping, sprint facilitation, and illustration.

During my free time, I'm drawing, learning tennis, and eating around Vancouver!

Feel free to send me an email or check out my resume for more detailed work experiences and skills.
