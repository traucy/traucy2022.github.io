Tracy Chen

Tracy never intended to be an artist. However, always wanting to freeze time, she set out to capture it through painting. In a society that champions speed and conformity, she finds solace in the little fleeting moments and pushing the boundaries of reality into the realm of the strangely familiar.

In 2022, Tracy graduated from Simon Fraser University with a major in Interactive Arts and Technology, and a minor in Publishing.

During 9am-5pm, Tracy is a senior product designer bringing artistry, strategy, and holistic thinking to every project.
