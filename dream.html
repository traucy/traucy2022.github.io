Problem

Creating art is time consuming, expensive, and intimidating.

Opportunity

By removing the barriers to art, how might we seamlessly encourage expression and creation?

Solution

Dream by WOMBO is an AI app that makes art creation easy for anyone.

Bring this idea from 0 to market within 3 months, the Dream app turns any prompt into beautiful stylized paintings within seconds.

My Role

On the product design team at Daybreak, I worked on Dream by WOMBO.

As one of the primary designers on Dream, I designed an intuituve artwork creation process, which was the main feature for the first version of Dream. The goal for Dream was to create a great product that did one thing extremely well.

I created information architecture flows, wireframes, visual moodboards, high fidelity UI flows, and prototypes. I worked with founders, developers, marketing leads, and designers on the WOMBO team, in addition to design managers from Daybreak.
