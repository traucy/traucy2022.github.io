Hi~! I’m Tracy, a product designer at Daybreak while studying design at the SFU’s School of Interactive Arts and Technology in Vancouver, Canada.
I excel in UI/visual design, interaction design, strategic thinking, prototyping, and illustration.

My ambition lies in understanding people, businesses, and non-humans to help create a holistic ecosystem of meaningful and purposeful interventions.
How people are moved by a product, how pixels are layed out, and the details that form each experience are what matters most to me.

During my free time, I study human anatomy, eat around Vancouver, and am doing my best to read this book about ways we could design for more than human-centered worlds.

Intro

On the product design team at Daybreak, I worked with Chipbrain, a company that designs the world's best communication-assistance AI to empower people with augmented empathy & persuasion in conversations.

At ChipBrain, they are inventing a world where people from different backgrounds can more easily relate to one another: when solving an argument with a partner, or selling a product to a customer, when asking your boss for a raise.

My Contribution

I was one of the primary product designers to redesign the desktop experience and innovate on the experience of online calls using systems thinking. I delivered high-fidelity desktop UI screens and prototyped high fidelity flows using Figma and After Effects.
